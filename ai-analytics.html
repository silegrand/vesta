The Challenge

The Limits of Traditional CCTV Are Now a Business Risk

A CCTV system that simply records is a liability management tool, not a security tool. By the time footage is reviewed, the event has long passed. Enterprise security demands systems that respond in real time.

Passive Recording Misses Events

Traditional CCTV records continuously but alerts on nothing. A perimeter breach at 3am, an unauthorised vehicle in a restricted area, an individual loitering near a server room — all recorded, none flagged.

Manual Review Takes Hours

Investigating an incident by scrubbing through hours of multi-camera footage is expensive, error-prone and slow. AI forensic search compresses hours of review into seconds of automated search.

Alert Fatigue from False Alarms

Basic motion detection generates constant false alerts from wind, headlights and wildlife. AI classification distinguishes people, vehicles and animals — dramatically reducing noise and restoring system confidence.

No Cross-Camera Correlation

Traditional CCTV cannot correlate events across cameras. AI analytics can track a person or vehicle across your entire camera estate simultaneously — impossible for any human operator to replicate.

Perimeter Security

Virtual Tripwires & Zone Intrusion

Draw detection lines or zones on any camera view. Any person or vehicle crossing a tripwire or entering a defined zone triggers an immediate alert with camera snapshot. Ideal for perimeter boundaries, restricted access areas and server rooms.

  • Define tripwires and zones on any camera in minutes
  • Direction-specific alerts — entry, exit, or both
  • Time-based rules — active only during defined hours
  • Immediate alert with camera snapshot to mobile or ARC
  • People and vehicle classification to reduce false alerts
Behaviour Detection

Loitering Detection & Crowd Analysis

Define a zone and a dwell-time threshold. Any person remaining beyond the defined period generates an alert. Effective for server rooms, car parks, building entrances and any area where prolonged presence is a security indicator.

  • Configurable dwell-time threshold per zone
  • Alert escalation for extended dwell events
  • Crowd density monitoring for occupancy management
  • Anti-social behaviour detection for public-facing areas
  • Integration with ARC monitoring for immediate response
