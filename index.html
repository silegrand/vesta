Who We Serve

Built for the Sectors
That Cannot Afford Downtime

Vesta IT brings enterprise-grade security and network infrastructure to the industries where reliable, compliant systems are not a preference — they are an operational requirement.

Transport & Shipping

Port operations, logistics hubs and transport depots demand perimeter CCTV, ANPR, access control and resilient connectivity across large, complex estates. We understand the operational pressures — and the regulatory requirements that come with critical national infrastructure.

Waste Management & Energy

Waste processing facilities and energy centres require robust CCTV across wide outdoor areas, vehicle access control and fire detection in environments where standard equipment cannot survive. We specify systems designed for the conditions, not against them.

Public Sector & Schools

Local authorities and educational establishments require compliant CCTV, controlled access and fire detection that meets public sector procurement standards and safeguarding obligations. We deliver systems that satisfy both the technology requirement and the governance framework around it.

Sports Stadiums

High-density crowd environments, high-value assets and strict licensing requirements make sports venues one of the most technically demanding CCTV and access control challenges. We design systems that meet sports ground licencing conditions and deliver evidential-quality footage when it is needed.

Construction

Temporary and permanent sites, off-grid locations and rapidly changing site layouts demand solar-powered CCTV, wireless connectivity and access control that moves with the project. We have deployed on sites with no mains power, no fixed infrastructure and no margin for security failure.

Finance Centres

Banks, insurance offices and financial services premises require layered physical security — controlled access, CCTV with compliant retention, and network infrastructure that meets FCA and PCI-DSS expectations. We understand the compliance environment, not just the technology.

John Bushnell

Director, Vesta IT Services Ltd

“Real infrastructure expertise isn’t learned in a classroom. It’s earned by keeping critical systems running — year after year — when the stakes are real and failure isn’t an option.”

John Bushnell’s career spans nearly three decades at the operational heart of enterprise IT. As Network Services Project Manager at Peel Ports Medway from 1995 to 2022, he was responsible for one of the most sustained infrastructure evolution programmes in the UK ports sector.

He arrived to find 10 computers and a single server. He left having designed, built, and managed a network of extraordinary scale — 50 buildings interconnected, more than 60 servers in production, and upwards of 250 clients dependent on the infrastructure he had created. The final chapter of his tenure was the most technically demanding: consolidating that entire estate into a centralised, high-efficiency WAN-style data centre.

That knowledge now underpins every Vesta IT project. When you engage us, you’re working with someone who has already solved problems at a scale most IT providers will never encounter.
