The Challenge

An Alarm Without Monitoring Is Just a Noise in an Empty Building

Corporate offices, industrial units and commercial properties sit unoccupied for the majority of the year — evenings, weekends, bank holidays and holiday periods. Without professional monitoring, a security event goes undetected until someone arrives the next morning.

Empty Buildings, Maximum Exposure

Commercial premises are most vulnerable when unoccupied — which is most of the time. A local alarm siren with no one to hear it provides minimal deterrence and zero response. Professional monitoring transforms passive hardware into active protection.

Insurer Requirements

Many commercial insurers require monitored intruder alarm systems as a condition of cover above certain value thresholds. NSI or SSAIB-approved ARC monitoring satisfies this requirement and may reduce your premium.

Keyholder Fatigue

Relying on individual staff to respond to every activation — including false alarms at 2am — is unsustainable. A professional ARC filters, verifies and only escalates genuine events, protecting your keyholders as well as your building.

Lone Worker Risk

Data centre engineers, caretakers, facilities staff and other lone workers in your buildings outside normal hours operate without a safety net. Lone worker monitoring provides automatic escalation if check-ins are missed.

Our Services

Core Service

Intruder Alarm Monitoring

Your intruder alarm panel connected to our NSI-approved ARC via secure dual-path signalling. Trained operators follow a defined response protocol — verifying the activation, contacting keyholders in sequence and notifying police under your site's URN for confirmed events.

  • NSI-approved ARC with 24/7/365 trained operator staffing
  • Dual-path signalling — broadband plus cellular backup
  • Keyholder notification in agreed priority sequence
  • Police response via URN for confirmed activations
  • Full activation reports for every event
Visual Verification

CCTV Remote Monitoring

IP CCTV cameras connected to the ARC allow operators to visually verify alarm activations before escalating — dramatically reducing false alarm police notifications while accelerating genuine response. Operators can view live feeds and challenge intruders via audio speakers.

  • Live camera access for visual verification of alarm events
  • Two-way audio challenge via on-site speakers
  • Reduces false police responses and protects your URN
  • Proactive monitoring during high-risk periods
  • Incident footage secured immediately for evidence
Life Safety

Fire Alarm Monitoring

Fire alarm panels connected to the ARC ensure immediate fire brigade notification on confirmed activation — even when the building is empty. For commercial premises where a fire could take hold overnight before discovery, this is the difference between a managed incident and a total loss.

  • Immediate fire brigade notification on confirmed activation
