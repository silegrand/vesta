The Challenge

Why Generic CCTV Installations Fail Enterprise Clients

Camera placement is only part of the challenge. Storage capacity, network load, remote access security and data retention compliance are equally critical — and rarely addressed properly by generic installers.

Wrong Camera for the Location

A camera specified without a site survey is a camera that will underperform. Wide dynamic range, focal length, low-light capability and vandal resistance must all be matched to the specific environment — not chosen from a catalogue.

Storage and Retention Failures

Insufficient NVR storage, incorrect retention periods and no audit trail for access are the most common compliance failures we encounter when inheriting systems from other installers. All are entirely preventable.

Insecure Remote Access

Default credentials on NVRs exposed directly to the internet is the norm, not the exception, in poorly specified systems. Enterprise remote access requires VPN, certificate-based authentication and regular security audit.

Network Impact Ignored

4K cameras generate substantial data — unplanned deployments saturate networks, degrade performance and cause recording gaps. Proper network design is inseparable from camera specification.
